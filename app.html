The first attempt was a soft decline (Do Not Honor), but Forter’s identity network already recognised this as a trusted customer rather than fraud. Instead of stopping at the decline, it re-presented the payment on a better performing processor and recovered the sale. A blind retry would gamble on every decline, whereas Forter pursues only the ones it knows are worth winning, so your authorization rate improves without inviting fraud back in.
